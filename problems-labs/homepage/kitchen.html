here you can learn about unorthodxed's go-to grub!

In case you haven't noticed, unorthodxed really loves chicken! It's his favorite meal, and he consumes some form of chicken almost every day. On the left we have his all time preference, the Buttermilk Fried Chicken from Bahama Breeze. Cooked to a crunchy crisp, it is accompanied by a lovely plethora of vegetables and mashed potatoes. On the right we have a more economically priced meal, the Popeyes tender combo meal. Plain and simple, Popeyes chicken gets the job done without the extra flair provided by a proper restaurant meal.


An honorable mention not pictured here is the spicy chicken sandwich from Chick-Fil-A. The zing it provides adds a special touch which is not present in either of unorthodxed's favorites pictured here. That's what earns it a spot as an option for a patron as picky as he.
